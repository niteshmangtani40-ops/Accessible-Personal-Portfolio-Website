My Story

Hello! I'm NItesh Mangtani, a passionate Computer Science student and Front-End Developer dedicated to crafting beautiful, highly accessible, and exceptionally performant web experiences. My journey in technology started with a deep curiosity about how software shapes our daily interactions, which quickly evolved into a dedicated pursuit of clean coding practices and web standards.
